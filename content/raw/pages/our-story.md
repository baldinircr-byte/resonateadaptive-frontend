# Ciao! My name is Riccardo, co-founder of Resonate!

I have been playing the piano since the age of 5.
Music is a fundamental part of who I am.

But in 2019 a spinal cord injury left me paralyzed from the waist down, changing every aspect of my life, including my ability to play the piano to its full potential. Without lower limb function, using the piano’s traditional pedal system was no longer possible.

For anyone, paralysis means the loss of many things, but for me, losing my ability to play the piano, the way I had always done, was one of the most difficult losses and something I was determined to overcome…

![Riccardo Baldini on stage during a concert in Edmonton](https://www.resonateadaptive.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/03/Riccardo-Baldini-receives-applause-2-683x1024.webp)

### Thus the idea for Resonate was born.

Working together with Steve Bosch, a Mechanical Design Engineer, RESONATE came to life!

In October of 2022, RESONATE made it possible for me to return to the stage for the first time since my injury – an unforgettable experience!

Everyone deserves to have equal access to music, and my hope is that RESONATE will become the gateway for anyone with limited lower limb function to access the full potential of the piano.

With your support, we can empower musicians, future musicians, and music lovers of all abilities to unlock their full potential; making the world a better place – one note at a time!
